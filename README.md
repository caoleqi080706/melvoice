# melvoice
此项目是一个高精度梅尔谱图项目，旨在通过分析梅尔谱图的基频以及泛音列表现，来综合分析目前发声所用音质以及发声方法，点击开始按钮，即可在下方观察梅尔谱图的波形表现。是目前主流声乐体系最常使用的声音分析图像。(例如在evt中)

This project is a high-precision mel spectrogram project, aiming to comprehensively analyze the current vocal quality and vocal technique used through the analysis of the fundamental frequency and harmonic list of the mel spectrogram. Click the "Start" button, and you can observe the waveform representation of the mel spectrogram below. It is the most commonly used sound analysis image in the current mainstream vocal music system. (For example, in evt)

此图表也可被称为声音光谱图(质谱仪)，它区别于普通的对数频谱图，能够更清晰直观的展示泛音列表现以及声音在各个频段的质量以及能量表现，相较于频谱图，能够更加直观的观察到声音在各频段的集中程度。

This chart can also be called a sound spectrogram (mass spectrometer). Different from an ordinary logarithmic spectrogram, it can more clearly and intuitively display the performance of the harmonic series and the quality and energy distribution of the sound in each frequency band. Compared with the spectrogram, it allows for a more direct observation of the concentration of the sound in various frequency ranges.
